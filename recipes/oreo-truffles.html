Oreo Truffles

They Seem So Fancy But Are So Easy

Every Christmas we make Oreo truffles. They are so simple and so delicious.They only take 3 ingredients, so invite your kids to help make them, too.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package Oreo cookies, crushed
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (24 ounce) package white chocolate bark
  • 1 (24 ounce) package chocolate bark

Steps

  1. Using a blender or hand held mixer, mix Oreos and cream cheese together. Roll into walnut size balls.
  2. Chill for an hour.
  3. Melt approximately 3/4 package of white almond bark. Stick a toothpick in an Oreo ball and dip it in the melted white almond bark. Allow to harden on wax paper. Wait about 15 minutes.
  4. While waiting, melt about 1/4 package of chocolate almond bark. When Oreo balls are no longer sticky to the touch, decorate with drizzles of chocolate and white almond bark.
